Game of Life

There's no time for more passwords now. You can see we have a gorgeous Game of Life on the background, initialized randomly. However, there are sometimes that the initial pattern is not as nice as we would like. We want to analyze the cycle length once it starts repeating, and also when does this cycle start, but we are having trouble calculating this, and we need your help.

You are given an initial Game of Life pattern as a matrix of 1s (alive cells) and 0s (dead cells). Calculate the iteration at which the cycle starts, and the length of it. Your solution should of the form (steps_to_cycle)-(cycle_length), without parenthesis. Keep in mind, that this Game of Life uses a looping matrix!
